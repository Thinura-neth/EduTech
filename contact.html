Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

+

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email. You'll receive a secure link to reset your password within minutes.

Can I download course materials?

+

Yes, most course materials are available for download. Look for the download icon in each lesson. Some materials might be restricted to online viewing only to protect intellectual property.

Do you offer certificates?

+

Yes, we provide certificates of completion for all courses upon successful finishing. Certificates are digitally signed and can be shared on LinkedIn and other professional networks.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for course payments. We also offer installment plans for higher-priced courses.
