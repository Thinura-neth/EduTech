Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course? +

You can enroll by visiting the Courses page, selecting your desired course, and clicking the "Enroll Now" button. You will need to be logged in to complete the process.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex) and local bank transfers. Our payment gateway is secured with modern encryption.

Do you offer certification? +

Yes, upon successful completion of any paid course, you will receive an official, verifiable digital certificate of completion from EduTech.
