Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans later?

+

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll get immediate access to new features. When downgrading, the changes take effect at the end of your billing cycle.

Is there a free trial?

+

All paid plans come with a 7-day free trial. You can explore all features risk-free. No credit card required for the Starter plan.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans.

Can I get a refund?

+

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all annual plans. If you're not satisfied, contact our support team for a full refund.

Do you offer student discounts?

+

Yes! Students can get 50% off any plan. Contact our support team with your student ID to get the discount applied.

Are there team plans available?

+

Absolutely! We offer custom team plans with additional features like progress tracking, team analytics, and dedicated account management. Contact sales for pricing.
